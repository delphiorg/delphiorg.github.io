In this episode we talk to <a href="http://www.monien.net/blog/">Olaf Monien</a> about the upcoming <a href="http://www.monien.net/blog/index.php/2009/01/delphilive-2009-physical-delphi-conference-in-the-us/">DelphiLive 2009 conference</a>, just announced on Thursday the 22nd, 2009.  The conference is organized by <a href="http://software-support.biz/zonen/sus/psecom,id,1,nodeid,1,p,0,_language,en.html">Software &amp; Support Verlag</a>, the usual sponsors of the <a title="EKON Conference" href="http://eurodevcon.com/">German Delphi conference EKON</a>.
<ul>
	<li><strong>Where</strong>: San Jose, CA, USA</li>
	<li><strong>When</strong>: May 13th – 16th 2009</li>
	<li><strong>Who</strong>: All Delphi developers, let it be Delphi Win 32, Delphi Prism or Delphi for PHP ones.</li>
</ul>
Stay tuned here and <a href="http://www.monien.net/blog/">Olaf's blog</a> for more information as it develops.
