<a href="http://www.MarcoCantu.com/"><img class="alignright" title="MarcoCantu" src="/assets/images/guests/MarcoCantu.jpg" alt="" width="136" height="148" /></a>Episode 10 is an interview with Spirit of Delphi Award Winner and definitive Delphi author Marco Cantu. This interview was recorded October 10th with Marco at his office in Italy.  We talk about Marco's new book, Delphi 2009 Features, REST application development, Domain Specific Languages, Generics, Anonymous Methods, Delphi Prism, Native Win32 vs. .NET Development and much more.
