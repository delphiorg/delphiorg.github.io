I exchanged emails with Marc-André Cournoyer of <a rel="nofollow" href="http://refactormycode.com/">RefactorMyCode.com</a> about supporting Delphi on his site. Since his site uses <a rel="nofollow" href="http://ultraviolet.rubyforge.org/">Ruby's UltraViolet</a> to do syntax highlighting, and it uses <a rel="nofollow" href="http://macromates.com/">TextMate</a> syntaxes, he needs a Delphi syntax for TextMate. Does anyone know where I might find one that has already been updated for Delphi? A Delphi Prism / Oxygene one would be great too!
