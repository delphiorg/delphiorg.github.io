<img class="size-full wp-image-2289 alignright" src="/assets/images/2016/03/Stefan-Glienke.jpg" alt="Stefan Glienke" width="165" height="220" />As a way to acknowledge some of the hard work and amazing contributions each of our MVPs provides we are starting a new MVP of the Year program. It is already a few months into 2016, but I would like to start by recognizing our MVP of the year for 2015, Stefan Glienke. <em>(He is missing from the <a href="http://www.embarcadero.com/mvp-directory">MVP directory</a>, but we will get him added right away.)</em>

Stefan started programming with Turbo Pascal over 15 years ago, and now uses Delphi as his productive language of choice. He is an expert in software design patterns, principles and practices. He developed the <a href="https://bitbucket.org/sglienke/dsharp">DSharp</a> library, <a href="https://bitbucket.org/sglienke/testinsight/wiki/Home">Test Insight</a> and is lead developer of <a href="https://bitbucket.org/sglienke/spring4d">Spring4d</a>.

The process of selecting an MVP of the year involves nominations and feedback from other MVPs as well as our internal consideration. Everyone holds Stefan in high regard and appreciates his many contributions. Here is a little about Stefan.
